About Me

Summary

I am a content creator with over 10,000+ followers across multiple platforms, passionate about uncovering and sharing the journeys of successful Pakistani entrepreneurs. My goal is to highlight hidden business personalities and inspire others through their stories. This focus has led me to collaborate with industry leaders like Faisal Aftab, a Shark Tank Pakistan investor and accomplished entrepreneur.

Beyond entrepreneurship, I create informative videos on a wide range of topics, including science facts, history, and current affairs. In addition to content creation, I am pursuing a degree in Data Science at Punjab University College of Information Technology (PUCIT).

As an aspiring data scientist, I aim to merge AI and economics to simplify financial decision-making and integrate AI with research for meaningful advancements. I am always open to networking, collaborations, and discussions about AI, economics, and entrepreneurship. Let's connect and explore opportunities together!
